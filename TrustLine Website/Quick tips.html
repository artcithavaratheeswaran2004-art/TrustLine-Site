Simple Tips to Stay Safe Online

A few simple habits can make a big difference when it comes to staying safe online. These are easy wins, no jargon, just practical advice.

  • 1 Use a strong and different password for each account.
  • 2 Think carefully before clicking links or downloading attachments from unexpected emails or messages.
  • 3 Turn on multi-factor authentication for extra protection on important accounts.
  • 4 Keep your phone, laptop, browser and apps up to date to reduce security risks.
  • 5 Be careful about what you share online, as personal information can be used in scams.
  • 6 Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive activity unless you are using a secure connection.
  • 7 Lock your screen whenever you leave your device unattended.
  • 8 Report suspicious emails, pop-ups or account activity as early as possible.

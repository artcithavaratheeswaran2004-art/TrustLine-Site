About TrustLine

Practical security guidance for smaller teams.

Covers GRC, ISO 27001 and the day-to-day habits that actually matter. Written for people who aren't security experts.

"We had no idea where to start with cyber security. TrustLine made it actually make sense."

~ Angel, Basecamp

What this is

TrustLine is a GRC awareness tool built as part of a university dissertation. It's aimed at small teams and organisations that don't have dedicated IT or security staff, and covers the kind of security basics that tend to get skipped because existing guidance is too technical or assumes too much.

Why it was built

Most security frameworks are written with large enterprises in mind. The checklists are long, the terminology is heavy, and very little of it translates to a team of five or ten people just trying to operate safely. This tool tries to fill that gap in a practical way.

What's covered

  • Governance, Risk and Compliance (GRC) basics
  • ISO 27001 explained without the enterprise framing
  • Security controls that are realistic for smaller teams
  • Getting staff to actually take security seriously
  • Day-to-day habits that make a real difference

A note on scope

TrustLine is not a full compliance audit tool and it won't replace a qualified security consultant if that's what you need. What it will do is give you a clear starting point, help you understand the terminology, and point you toward the right areas to focus on first.
